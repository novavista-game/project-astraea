Global Ingress Evaluation Metrics

Standardizing structural audits in compliance with UN-Habitat development guidelines, KOICA international assistance policies, and Green Climate Fund (GCF) climate-resilience vulnerability frameworks.

01

Roofing Condition

Analyzes degradation index, sagging supports, structural rot, and material classification (asbestos sheet vs. corrugated iron vs. concrete slab).

02

Water & Sanitation Ingress

Estimates drinking water coordinates distance and tracks physical proximity to open sewer runoffs and waste channels.

03

Sanitary Latrine

Identifies presence of segregated toilets, closed drainage connection, and septic tank integration to mitigate disease transmission.

04

Floor Composition

Detects raw dirt flooring vs. concrete paving to calculate risk indexes for hookworm, sand-fly, and parasitic infection vectors.

05

Kitchen Infrastructure

Evaluates ventilation efficiency, range hood functionality, and electrical/gas layout proximity to water sources to compute localized indoor air quality risks and fire hazard mitigation indexes.

THE DEPLOYED BACKEND

Technical Audacity

Astraea synthesizes raw edge captures and satellite data into verifiable impact outputs. By matching **Sentinel-2** 10-meter bands (B4, B8, B11) with local photos, our super-resolution neural network resolves boundaries to a virtual 0.5-meter grid.

Upon image ingest, the **Gemini 3.5 Flash** VLM computes the Healthspan Environmental Vulnerability Index (HEVI), generating a cryptographic proof on-chain to trigger payments.

  • AstraeaGrantEscrowVault.sol: Overrides standard withdraws to restrict liquidity pathways.
  • zk-SNARK Containment Circuits: Hides GPS inputs while proving H3 cell coordinates.
