THE DEPLOYED BACKEND

Technical Audacity

Astraea synthesizes raw edge captures and satellite data into verifiable impact outputs. By matching **Sentinel-2** 10-meter bands (B4, B8, B11) with local photos, our super-resolution neural network resolves boundaries to a virtual 0.5-meter grid.

Upon image ingest, the **Gemini 3.5 Flash** VLM computes the Healthspan Environmental Vulnerability Index (HEVI), generating a cryptographic proof on-chain to trigger payments.
