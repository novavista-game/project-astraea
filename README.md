# Project Astraea: The Unbanked Real Estate Formalization Engine

Project Astraea is a high-audacity Moonshot initiative engineered for the X-Prize competition. Its primary mission is the systemic economic formalization and tokenization of unregistered land and property assets for the 70% of the global population lacking formal deeds.

By synthesizing multi-modal smartphone imagery, edge-processed drone captures, and multi-spectral satellite telemetry, Project Astraea resolves the "Unbanked Real Estate" dilemma. It establishes an immutable digital land deed clone, mapping physical structures to decentralized ledgers to provide proof of asset equity and unlock micro-finance inclusion.

## Technical Architecture Overview
1. **Multimodal VLM Structural Analytics**: Visual analytics engine utilizing advanced Vision-Language Models (e.g., Gemini Flash) to extract structural metrics, degradation indices, and material quality scores.
2. **Geo-Spatial Data Fusion Pipeline**: A decoupled data ingestion pipeline combining GPS coordinates, camera azimuth, OpenStreetMap, and ESA Sentinel imagery to resolve geographic coordinates without standard postal addresses.
3. **Decentralized Economic Tokenization Pathways**: Algorithmic "Asset Credit Score" calculations integrated with a Stateless Tokenization Proxy and decentralized public ledgers to register property assets.
